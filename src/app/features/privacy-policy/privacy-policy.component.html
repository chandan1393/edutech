How we collect, use, and protect your personal information

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you register, we collect your full name, email address, and phone number. This information is used solely for account management and service delivery.

Class Credentials

To complete your online class assignments, we collect your institution portal URL, username, and password. This information is:

  • Stored encrypted in our database
  • Only accessible to the assigned expert writer and our admin team
  • Never shared with third parties
  • Deleted upon your request or account closure
🔐 Your portal credentials are handled with the highest security standards. Writers only receive access after explicit admin approval.

Payment Information

Payments are processed by Stripe. We do not store your credit card numbers or banking details. We only store confirmation records of completed payments.

Usage Data

We collect anonymous usage events (page views, feature usage) to improve our service. This data is not linked to personally identifiable information for external reporting.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To deliver the online class completion service you enrolled for
  • To send payment confirmations and assignment delivery notifications
  • To provide customer support and respond to your queries
  • To improve our platform through anonymous usage analytics
  • To send WhatsApp callbacks only if you explicitly request them

3. Cookies

We use the following cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: Authentication token (JWT), session ID — required for the service to function
  • Analytics Cookies: Anonymous session tracking to understand how users navigate our platform

We do NOT use advertising cookies or share data with ad networks. You can accept all cookies or essential-only cookies via our cookie consent banner.

4. Data Sharing

We share your data with:

  • Stripe: Payment processing (see Stripe Privacy Policy)
  • Twilio: WhatsApp messaging (only if you request a callback)
  • Assigned Expert Writers: Course name, description, and portal credentials (after explicit admin approval)

We do NOT sell your personal data to any third party.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion:

  • Portal credentials are permanently deleted within 24 hours
  • Payment records are retained for 7 years for tax compliance
  • Communication logs are retained for 2 years

6. Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update incorrect information from your dashboard
  • Deletion: Request account and data deletion
  • Portability: Export your data in a machine-readable format
  • Withdrawal: Withdraw consent for analytics at any time

7. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • All data in transit is encrypted using TLS 1.3
  • Passwords are hashed using BCrypt with salt rounds
  • JWT tokens expire after 24 hours
  • Rate limiting prevents brute-force attacks (5 login attempts per minute per IP)
  • Portal credentials are stored encrypted at rest
