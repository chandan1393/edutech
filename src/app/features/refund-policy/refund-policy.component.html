Refund & Revision Policy

EduPilotHelp is committed to delivering results that meet or exceed the agreed standard on every class. This policy explains exactly what happens when we fall short.

Last updated: January 2025 · EduPilotHelp

1. Our Core Commitment

EduPilotHelp operates a pay-as-you-go installment model specifically to protect students. You never pay for work that has not been delivered and admin-reviewed. This structure is our primary consumer protection mechanism.

2. Unlimited Revisions

All plans include unlimited revisions within 7 days of each deliverable. If you are not satisfied with any assignment, paper, or project, simply request a revision through your student dashboard and specify the required changes. We will complete revisions within 24–48 hours at no additional charge.

3. Late Delivery Policy

If EduPilotHelp fails to deliver any agreed deliverable by the stated deadline, the corresponding installment payment is automatically waived. You will not be charged for work that was not delivered on time. This commitment is unconditional.

4. Full Refunds — Eligible Situations

  • Class has not started and expert has not been assigned
  • EduPilotHelp cancels the service due to inability to source a qualified expert
  • Payment processed but onboarding not completed within 48 hours
  • Duplicate payments made in error

5. Partial Refunds

Partial refunds may be issued where a portion of the class has been completed. The refundable amount will be calculated based on work completed versus the total agreed installment value.

6. Non-Refundable Situations

  • Class has started and work is in progress
  • Student changes their mind after expert assignment
  • Student provided incorrect class details resulting in mismatched delivery
  • Grade outcomes that meet our stated B-or-better standard but are below student expectation

7. How to Request a Refund or Revision

Contact our support team via your student dashboard, WhatsApp, or email at support@edupilothelp.com. All refund requests are reviewed and responded to within 24 hours. Approved refunds are processed within 5–10 business days via the original Stripe payment method.

8. Grade Disputes

If the final grade for any delivered class falls below the agreed standard, we will provide additional work, retakes, or supplementary material at no charge. We are committed to your academic outcome from first assignment to final grade.

9. Contact

For all refund and revision queries: support@edupilothelp.com
