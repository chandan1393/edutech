Academic Integrity Policy

EduPilotHelp provides academic support, tutoring, and reference materials. Understanding how our service aligns with academic support principles is important to us.

Last updated: January 2025 · EduPilotHelp

1. Our Position on Academic Support

EduPilotHelp operates as an academic support and tutoring platform. We provide reference materials, model answers, expert coaching, and academic guidance to assist students in understanding their coursework. Our service is analogous to working with a private tutor, academic coach, or study group.

2. How Students Should Use Our Service

Students using EduPilotHelp are responsible for understanding how to appropriately incorporate the academic guidance and reference materials we provide into their own learning and coursework submissions, in accordance with their institution's policies and academic integrity standards.

3. Plagiarism-Free Commitment

All content produced by EduPilotHelp experts is original and written from scratch for each student. We use industry-standard plagiarism detection tools — the same tools used by major universities — to verify originality before every delivery. We never recycle content between students.

4. Original Work Standards

  • All assignments and papers are written freshly for each individual student
  • Content is verified through plagiarism detection before delivery
  • Writers do not share or reuse content across multiple students
  • All sources are properly cited in the required citation format (APA, MLA, Chicago)

5. Student Responsibility

Students engaging with EduPilotHelp agree to use delivered content responsibly and in compliance with their institution's guidelines. EduPilotHelp is not responsible for how students choose to use the academic reference materials and support provided.

6. Our Ethical Stance

EduPilotHelp believes that access to quality academic support should not be limited by financial resources or geographic location. We are committed to helping every student access expert guidance that enables better learning outcomes, improved understanding, and successful academic completion.
